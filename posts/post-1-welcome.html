Welcome to AzureCraft

AzureCraft exists to capture practical Azure architecture knowledge: the kind learned through real design decisions, tradeoffs, diagrams, and repeatable customer-safe patterns.

What this site is for

This is a field-notes library rather than a news feed. Each article should help an architect explain a pattern, make a decision, or start a better conversation about production-ready Azure design.

What you will find here

  • Azure Landing Zone foundations and governance patterns.
  • Data and AI architectures with a focus on operational readiness.
  • Modern application patterns that connect platform, security, and delivery thinking.
  • Reusable checklists and diagrams for community sessions and customer-safe conversations.
Editorial principle: explain the pattern, show the tradeoff, and leave the reader with something they can reuse.

How to use it

Start with the GenAIOps CSA guide if you are working on AI adoption, or open the pattern library if you need a visual starting point for an architecture conversation.
